Week Two in Scotland

Tue 16 April 2013 | tags: BVCCKayakingWhite Water

Well week 2 wasn't much different to week 1, with there still being no water in Scotland. As a result much of the time was spent walking and sight seeing rather than kayaking.

We paddled the River Awe, which allowed us to see some interesting signs, hide Richards boat in the middle of the river and experience a bit of white water.

Luckily the River Garry was releasing again this week, which gave me opportunity to get my head wet, as well as practice my air brace.

Photo by Aoife Strahan

Photo By Phil Porter

Apart from that there was just much driving, walking, sight seeing and general relaxing at the spa - Certainly not the 2 weeks away that I had planned in my mind but a relaxing holiday instead. Lets just hope for some more water next time!
