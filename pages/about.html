About

So I'm an Electronic Engineer, who now spends most of his time doing DSP and Signal processing at work. I spend a lot of time working with MATLAB and Simulink in my day job as a "Systems Engineer" for a defence company. Outside of work you will find my experimenting with Software Defined Radio (SDR), open source FPGA boards, and 3D Printing.

I studied Electronic Engineering at University of Surrey in Guildford, and graduated in 2013. I'm originally from Worcestershire, and I attended Worcester Sixth Form College where I studied Math, Physics Electronics and computing before heading off to university.

In my free time I enjoy Kayaking, and Mountain Biking. A new sport I have recently taken up is indoor rock climbing.

I've recently started trying to put more of my little home projects up on github, we will see if i'm successful and the graph below becomes a bit more green.
